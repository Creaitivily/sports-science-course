## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

### Vision
Build a sustainable creator business around evidence-based sports science education, culminating in the sale of a high-ticket comprehensive course while generating diversified revenue streams through content creation and audience building.

### End Goal
Sell the **Sports Science Certification Course** (5 Disciplines, 31 Modules, ~52,000 words) as a high-ticket product ($997-$2,997) to coaches, trainers, and sports science professionals.

### Unique Value Proposition
- **Academic Rigor + Practical Application** — Bridge between university education and gym-floor coaching
- **Instructor's Research** — Original research on sleep-training interactions (Daubian-Nosé, 2025)
- **Coach-Focused** — Target underserved market of coaches wanting science, not just athletes
- **Integrated Approach** — Cross-discipline connections (training × sleep × psychology × assessment)
- **Portuguese/Brazilian Market** — Potential for bilingual content in underserved market
