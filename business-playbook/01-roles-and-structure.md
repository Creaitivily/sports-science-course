# Roles & Structure
## The Visionary vs. Integrator Model

---

## The Partnership Dynamic

This is often referred to as:
- **Visionary vs. Integrator** (EOS model)
- **Talent vs. Manager/Producer** (Creator economy)

---

## Person A: The Face

### Role
The on-camera talent. The brand identity.

### Responsibilities
- Bring high energy and charisma
- Provide raw expertise/knowledge
- Execute the scripts provided by Person B

### Primary Metric
**Quality of delivery on camera**

### Time Commitment
- Batch filming sessions (e.g., every Tuesday morning)
- Weekly sync meetings

---

## Person B: The Architect

### Role
Remove all friction from the content creation process. Translate creative chaos into a predictable business asset.

### Responsibilities
- Decide **what** is filmed
- Decide **how** it is edited/packaged
- Build the automations that distribute content

### Primary Metrics
- Total Views
- Posting Consistency
- Process Efficiency

### Mindset
> Person A should never have to guess what to say. Person B provides the hook, the talking points, and the call to action (CTA).

---

## Summary Table

| Aspect | Person A (Face) | Person B (Architect) |
|--------|-----------------|---------------------|
| **Focus** | Content delivery | Systems & strategy |
| **Creates** | The videos | The scripts & workflows |
| **Controls** | Energy & expertise | Calendar & automations |
| **Measures** | On-camera quality | Views & efficiency |
