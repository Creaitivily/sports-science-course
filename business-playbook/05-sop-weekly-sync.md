# SOP: Weekly Sync Meeting

The most critical meeting of the week where Person B hands off the strategy to Person A.

---

## Meeting Details

| Aspect | Recommendation |
|--------|----------------|
| **Duration** | 30-45 minutes max |
| **Frequency** | Weekly (same day/time) |
| **Format** | Video call or in-person |
| **Owner** | Person B runs the agenda |

---

## Agenda Template

### 1. Review (10 min)
**Purpose:** What happened last week?

- [ ] Top performing content (show the data)
- [ ] What formats worked?
- [ ] What topics resonated?
- [ ] Comments/DMs worth noting

### 2. Feedback (5 min)
**Purpose:** Quick wins and adjustments

- [ ] "Do more of X" (specific format/topic)
- [ ] "Stop doing Y" (what's not working)
- [ ] Energy/delivery notes if needed

### 3. Handoff (15 min)
**Purpose:** Next week's content

- [ ] Review the "Shooting Packet" for next batch
- [ ] Walk through each script/brief
- [ ] Clarify any questions Person A has
- [ ] Confirm filming date/time

### 4. Ideas (5 min)
**Purpose:** Capture Person A's brain dump

- [ ] Any trending topics noticed?
- [ ] Questions from the audience?
- [ ] Personal stories to share?

---

## Preparation Checklist

### Person B (Before Meeting)
- [ ] Pull last week's analytics
- [ ] Prepare shooting packet (5-10 scripts)
- [ ] Note top comments/engagement
- [ ] Update content calendar

### Person A (Before Meeting)
- [ ] Watch back one video from last week
- [ ] Note any ideas from the week
- [ ] Clear schedule for batch filming day

---

## Post-Meeting Actions

| Who | Action | Deadline |
|-----|--------|----------|
| Person B | Share finalized shooting packet | Same day |
| Person B | Update project management tool | Same day |
| Person A | Confirm filming availability | Within 24h |
