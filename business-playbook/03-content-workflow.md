# The Content Factory Workflow

A cyclical 5-phase workflow to keep the business moving.

---

## Phase 1: Ideation
**Owner:** Person B + Automation

| Action | Output |
|--------|--------|
| Set up RSS feed or AI agent to scrape news/trends | List of 20 potential video ideas reviewed weekly |

---

## Phase 2: Pre-Production
**Owner:** Person B

| Action | Output |
|--------|--------|
| Select best 5 ideas and write scripts/outlines | "Shooting Packet" ready for Person A |

---

## Phase 3: The Shoot
**Owner:** Person A

| Action | Output |
|--------|--------|
| Film all 5 videos in one batch session | Raw video files uploaded to cloud |

**Best Practice:** Schedule a recurring "batch day" (e.g., every Tuesday morning)

---

## Phase 4: Post-Production
**Owner:** Person B (or outsourced editor)

| Action | Output |
|--------|--------|
| Edit footage and create thumbnails | Finished assets |

---

## Phase 5: Distribution
**Owner:** Automated

| Action | Output |
|--------|--------|
| Content scheduled via API/software | Videos go live on TikTok, YouTube, IG, LinkedIn simultaneously |

---
